# Core Functions & Services

## Account & Deposit Services
- Current accounts (conventional, then increasingly Islamic/Shariah-compliant)
- Savings accounts
- Term deposits / investment certificates
- Business and SME accounts
- Remittance-focused accounts for overseas Pakistanis

## Financing & Lending
- Consumer finance (personal loans, auto finance, home finance)
- Corporate lending and syndicated finance
- SME working capital and trade finance
- Islamic financing modes (Murabaha, Ijarah, Diminishing Musharakah, etc.) introduced progressively through the Islamic conversion process

## Cards & Payments
- Debit and credit card issuance
- Point-of-sale (POS) services for merchants
- Bill payment services

## Trade & Corporate Services
- Letters of credit and guarantees
- Foreign exchange services
- Cash management solutions for corporate clients

## Treasury Functions
- Liquidity management
- Investment in government securities and money market instruments
- Foreign exchange trading desk

## Wealth & Investment Services
- Priority/wealth banking for high-net-worth individuals
- Investment certificates and asset management partnerships

## Remittance Services
Faysal Bank has historically positioned itself among the top banks in Pakistan's home remittance market, offering dedicated accounts and services for overseas Pakistanis sending money home.

## Customer Support Functions
- Branch network services
- Call center / phone banking
- Complaint resolution processes, including escalation paths to the State Bank of Pakistan's "Sunwai" complaint portal

## How Functions Shifted During 2010–2020

| Function Area | Pre-2010 State | Post-2020 State |
|---|---|---|
| Product base | Conventional banking products | Predominantly Shariah-compliant products |
| Branch network | Smaller, pre-acquisition footprint | Significantly expanded (RBS acquisition + organic growth) |
| Compliance focus | Standard regulatory compliance | Heightened focus following 2020 SBP fine |
| Customer channels | Branch-centric | Beginning of digital/self-service channel development |
